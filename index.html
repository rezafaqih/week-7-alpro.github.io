About Us

Suprek is a renowned chicken shop that takes pride in serving delicious, high-quality chicken dishes across Indonesia. With a commitment to freshness and flavor, Mr. Suprek has become a favorite spot for chicken lovers nationwide. Whether dining in or ordering for delivery, customers can always expect fast service and the best chicken dishes that keep them coming back for more.
